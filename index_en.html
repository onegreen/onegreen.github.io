Environmental licensing

Currently the licensing process is quite complex and the trend is that environmental agencies are increasingly stringent in their requirements. Therefore, it is vital to have the support of information technology to modernize and manage the process, making it more agile.

Simplifying the licensing process

During the licensing process, a missed deadline or requirement not met can compromise the operation of an enterprise, resulting in delays, increased costs and damaging the profitability of the business. onegreen was developed based on the methodology GTD (Getting Things Done), making the environmental licensing process shared between the area that requires licensing and environmental area, getting clear responsibility of each to the process.

  1. Requirements Management
  2. ROI Optimization
  3. Reduce Costs
  4. Locate Documents

Requirements Management

Eliminate delays and rework while you decentralizes control requirements. Document execution and, with just one click, generate all the evidence of compliance report of the requirements of a license.

ROI Optimization

Reduce the risks and delays in the licensing process that can lead to a postponement of the start of production and, therefore, an impairment of generation of a development box.

Reduce Costs

Reduce costs with environmental management systematizing the collection, organization, analysis, sharing and monitoring information. Optimize the work of environmental team, directing its efforts to the management and decision-making and not the generation of information and reports.

Locate Documents

Keep safe and accessible environmental memory of the company, to organize and store in digital media, documents generated throughout the licensing process of an enterprise. Eliminate thus the need to find a document in the physical files of the company.
